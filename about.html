Debidwar Govt Reaz Uddin Pilot Model High School

Nawab Kazi Golam Mohiuddin Faroqui Founded This School in The Name Of His Father Kazi Reaz Uddin Ahmed Faroqui in 1918 AD. The Main Campus Of The School is 6 Acres. ABM Golam Mustafa Stadium is Also Owned By The School. Although The School Started As an Boys School From Its Inception; In 2011, After 91 Years Of Establishment, The School Became a Model School Under MPO and Started Admitting Female Students. Currently The School Offers Both Male/Female Studies. The Governmentalization Of This School Was Completed in 2016 As Part Of the Government's “Every Upzila Has At Least One Government High School” Project.

The School Was Established in 1918. As a Result, The School Completed its Centenary Year in 2018. On March 3, 2018,The Centenary Of The School Was Celebrated With a Grand Function in The School Premises. Finance Minister AHM Mustafa Kamal Was Present as the chief guest. Former Deputy Minister Fakhrul Islam Munshi and MP Razi Mohammad Fakhrul Were Also Present. Apart From This, The Current Students Of The School and The Alumni of All The Batches Were Also Present. On This Day, The School Premises Became a Meeting Place.

Debidwar Government Reaz Uddin Pilot Model High School is Located at Sadar of Debidwar Upazila in Comilla District. This Centenary Old School is One Of The Oldest Educational Institutions. It is Located in The Heart Of Debidwar City, Next To The Debidwar Police Station And Post Office. This School Offers Two Shifts - Morning and Afternoon. It's a High School; The Program Spans From The 6th Standard To The 10th Standard. Currently, There Are About 2000+ Students Studying At The School.
